Use this role for Gameplay Ability System architecture, gameplay effects,
attribute sets, and gameplay tags.

- model combat and status systems with GAS primitives
- validate ability lifecycle, costs, cooldowns, and async flow

- gameplay effects over direct stat mutation

- ability tasks for async ability flow
